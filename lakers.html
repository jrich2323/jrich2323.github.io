  • NBA Championship (2020)Led the Lakers to their 17th NBA title, tying them with the Boston Celtics for the most in league history.The championship came during the COVID-19 pandemic in the NBA “bubble” in Orlando. Won the NBA Finals MVP, becoming the first player in NBA history to win Finals MVP with three different franchises (Cleveland, Miami, L.A.).
  • All-Star Selections Selected to the All-Star Game every year as a Laker. Served as team captain multiple times due to being the top vote-getter in the Western Conference.
  • Became the NBA’s All-Time Leading Scorer In February 2023, LeBron passed Kareem Abdul-Jabbar to become the NBA’s all-time leading scorer, a monumental career milestone.
  • Consistent Elite Play Despite his age, LeBron has maintained All-NBA level performance, averaging 25–30+ points per game in multiple seasons. Played a major role in keeping the Lakers competitive in the Western Conference.
  • Western Conference Finals (2023) Led the Lakers to the Western Conference Finals in 2023 after a strong playoff run, though they were swept by the Denver Nuggets.
