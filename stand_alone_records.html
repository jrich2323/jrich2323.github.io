  • All-Time Leading Scorer Most career points in NBA history (regular season), surpassing Kareem Abdul-Jabbar in 2023. He is also the only player to score over 40,000 total career points (including playoffs and regular season).
  • Only Player with 30K Points, 10K Rebounds, and 10K Assists LeBron is the only player in NBA history to reach 30,000+ points, 10,000+ rebounds, and 10,000+ assists, showing his all-around excellence.
  • Most All-NBA Team Selections LeBron has the most All-NBA selections in history (currently 19 as of 2024), more than any other player.
  • Most Playoff Points in NBA History LeBron holds the record for most total points in NBA playoff history, surpassing Michael Jordan.
  • Only Player to Win Finals MVP with 3 Different Teams He is the only player ever to win NBA Finals MVP with three different franchises: Miami Heat (2012, 2013) Cleveland Cavaliers (2016) Los Angeles Lakers (2020)
  • Most Consecutive NBA Finals Appearances in the Modern Era (8 straight) From 2011 to 2018, LeBron reached 8 consecutive NBA Finals (4 with Miami, 4 with Cleveland), unmatched in the modern era.
  • First and Only Player to Score 10+ Points in 1,000+ Consecutive Games LeBron has scored at least 10 points in over 1,100 consecutive games, the longest streak in NBA history.
